We collect paper, you give it a second life

Template Note creates high quality notebooks & notepads using only excess paper Excess paper: A lot of paper scraps are left lying around after printing jobs. These excesses are impractical for the printers use and most of it ends up being recycled. However, the best ecological solution is to reuse the paper as is.
